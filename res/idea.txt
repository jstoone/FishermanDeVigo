Follow the daily routine of Abel Pescadoado, a fisherman who lives in Vigo with his wife, son and daughter.
Use knowledge and tact to choose the best place to fish, and to get the finest fish.
Survive the depression of 1930, last ten years until the economy picks up or lose the game.
During the day, move around the coast finding the best places to fish, control the quality of fish and stop diseased fish being passed on.
During the night, manage your families finances, keep them fed, buy new boats and fishing gear.
